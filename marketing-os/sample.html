Excavation Contractor in Utah — Toone Construction Co.

Meta description: Toone Construction Co. handles commercial site work, mass excavation, and utilities across Utah. Family-run since 1990. Call for a bid.

We move dirt across Utah for commercial pads, subdivisions, and public work. Four generations on the same yard in Mapleton, Utah. If it involves a track hoe, a pay item, and a tight schedule, we're at home.

Scope: What We Self-Perform

Mass excavation, stripping topsoil to subgrade, cut-fill balance with GPS-guided iron, structural fill placement, storm and sewer mains, and import/export when the site demands it. We coordinate joint trench with Rocky Mountain Power and Dominion Energy directly so your dry-utility schedule doesn't slip.

What Utah GCs Get When We're On The Job

A foreman who reads plans before he reads email. Equipment that starts in February. A super who'll tell you the truth about schedule on a Tuesday morning, not Friday afternoon. And a turnover that doesn't leave a punch list for the concrete crew.

Working in Utah: Geography & Reg Notes

Soils across Utah run the spectrum — alluvial fans on the bench, high water table near the lake, and rock once you climb east. We factor frost depth, UDOT permit lead times, and city-specific SWPPP requirements into the schedule from day one. No surprises at preconstruction.

Frequently asked

Are you bonded and insured for public work?
Yes — bonded and insured for public and private work across Utah, with current UDOT prequalification. We can send a current bonding letter same day.
What's your typical mobilization lead time?
Two to four weeks for most commercial pads, faster for repeat GC clients. Subdivision starts we plan with a longer runway because of utility coordination.
Do you self-perform wet utilities or sub it out?
Self-perform. Storm, sewer, and water mains are our crews and our iron. That's how we keep the schedule honest.
